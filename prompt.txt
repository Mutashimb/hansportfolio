Create a modern, bright, and energetic personal portfolio website with a strong geometric theme.

Main design specifications:
- Primary background color: pure white (#FFFFFF) or very light off-white (#FAFAFA) for the entire site
- Vibrant accent colors: bright red (#FF3B30 or #E63946), bright yellow (#FFCA3A or #FFD60A), bright green (#00FF9F or #06D6A0), bright blue (#3A86FF or #4361EE). Use all four colors balanced across the design — do not let one color dominate.
- Core design elements: geometric shapes (circles, triangles, rounded rectangles, diagonal lines, overlapping abstract forms, geometric patterns). Use them for:
  - Hero section decoration
  - Section dividers
  - Very subtle background pattern (5–10% opacity)
  - Hover effects on cards/projects
  - Icons, bullet points, decorative elements
- Overall feeling: playful yet professional, clean & minimal but not boring, very modern 2025–2026 aesthetic, fully mobile-responsive, smooth micro-interactions (on scroll & hover)

Navigation (sticky/fixed navbar at the top) with these exact menu items:
1. CV
2. Education
3. Portfolio
4. Publications
5. Skills

Desired page structure & section order (single-page scroll or multi-page with smooth navigation):

1. Hero Section (topmost)
   - Large full name + professional tagline (e.g. "Hanifan Mu'tashim | [Your Profession]")
   - Circular or geometric-framed profile photo
   - Colorful CTA buttons (e.g. "View Portfolio" or "Contact Me") using rotating accent colors
   - Background: white + subtle floating geometric shapes or light overlay

2. CV Section
   - Concise work experience summary in vertical timeline or geometric cards
   - Use geometric icons + different accent colors per entry

3. Education Section
   - List of degrees, institutions, years, GPA (if relevant)
   - Display as cards or timeline with colored accent blocks on the side

4. Portfolio Section (most important section)
   - Grid or masonry layout for projects (3–4 columns on desktop, 1–2 on mobile)
   - Each project: large thumbnail + geometric shape overlay on hover
   - Project title, short description, tech stack (pill-shaped tags with varied accent colors)
   - Optional category filters (e.g. UI/UX, Web Development, Graphic Design, etc.)

5. Publications Section
   - List of papers, journals, conferences, articles
   - Include title, year, link (if available), venue/publisher
   - Use accent colors for numbering, icons, or highlight bars

6. Skills Section
   - Display skills using geometric progress bars, circular charts, or sized tag cloud (size = proficiency level)
   - Group into categories: Programming, Design Tools, Soft Skills, etc.
   - Different accent colors per category

Additional desired features:
- Simple footer: copyright, social media icons (geometric style), email, LinkedIn/WhatsApp links
- Smooth animations: fade-in on scroll, slight scale on card hover, subtle moving geometric shapes in hero background
- Typography: modern sans-serif fonts (e.g. Inter, Poppins, Manrope, or Geist); bold playful headings + clean body text
- Perfectly responsive on mobile & tablet
- Keep it clean — avoid clutter; focus remains on content

Overall inspiration: create a portfolio style commonly seen on Dribbble / Awwwards — geometric colorful accents on white background, very bright and energetic yet still professional.
